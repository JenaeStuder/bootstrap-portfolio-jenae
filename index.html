About Me

I'm a little bit shy and more than a little bit snarky. I enjoy reading poetry, and anything that either makes you think or has a good story line. I love music and most days can't function without it playing somewhere near me. I enjoy all genres of music from showtunes to r&b, especially live music and the theater. One day I hope to have the free time to enjoy season passes to Broadway Across America. I like working out and can be often found in classes that have some type of dance theme. I love exploring new things and expanding my knowledge, be it somewhere new to eat, a new place to discover or even learning a new skill. I'm always up for an adventure and can often be found looking for something new to do. I don't like to be idle and thanks to my favorite people on the planet, I rarely have the time to be. They like to keep me on my toes and constantly keep me challenged and growing.
